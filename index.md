# mocha-theme
*mocha-theme* is a [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com) theme made for people who like the colors of coffee/mocha.

'Enjoy a hot cup of mocha, just for you!'

##### Short Showcase

A recipe for a *nice* mocha!

Ingredients:
* 1 cup of hot brewed coffee
* 1 tablespoon of a unsweetened cocoa powder
* 1 tablespoon of white sugar
* 2 tablespoons of milk

1. Gather your ingredients!
2. Pour hot coffee into a mug. Stir in your coca, sugar and milk.
3. **Enjoy!**



I hope you enjoy this theme! :heart:
